Our Story

Founded in March 16 2011, Travel Spot is a company with the passion of traveling and commitment in showcasing the beauty of destinations in the African continent. Our mission was and still is to provide travelers with authentic experiences and valuable insights that enrich their journey's. Driven by a love for exploration and desire to promote sustainable tourism, we provide unique content that highlights local cultures and responsible travel practices. as we continue to grow, we remain dedicated to fostering meaningful connections between travelers and the communities they visit to ensure that each journey leaves a positive impact

We are passionate travelers who want to share the beauty of Africa with you.

Our purpose and vision

The purpose of TravelSpot is to empower travelers by providing them with authentic, insightful and practical information about Africa's diverse destinations. Our vision is to create a global community of travelers who appreciate the beauty of Africa and prioratise responsible travel.
